Types of Threats

- Deviations in QoS

- Espionage or Trespass

#### Espionage or Trespass

  - Rainbow tables
  - Man-in-the-middle
  - Key loggers
  - Social Engineering
 
#### Software Attacks
- Malware
  - Viruses & Worms
  - Clickbait
  - Trojan Horses
- Back doors
- Denial of Service (DoS) & Distributed DoS
- Spam
- DNS Cache Poisoning
